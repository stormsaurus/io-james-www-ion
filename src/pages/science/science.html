Science

I used to do experimental Physical Chemistry. It's between physics and chemistry. Eventually I changed paths and arranged bits instead of molecules.

My Ph.D. research was at the University of California Los Angeles, undergraduate research was at University of Florida Gainesville, while also spending some time at L.U.R.E. which was a synchrotron facility in Orsay, France. We published some research about two dimensional monolayers which were subjected to crushing barriers, lasers and particle accelerators.

The molecules didn't have a chance.

A high power laser microscope images of single molecular layers on water. If you catch them at just the right conditions they create some fantastic images.
